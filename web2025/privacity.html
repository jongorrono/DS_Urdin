    Privacy Policy

    Welcome! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit or use the services on jongodesign.com. We've written it in plain English so it's easy to understand—no legal jargon here.

    1. Who's Responsible for Your Data?

    JongoDesign, operated by Jon Gorroño, located in Durango, Basque Country, Spain.

    Email: jon@jongodesign.com

    Phone: +34 671 467 834

    2. Why We Use Your Data

    We process your data for the following purposes:

    • Responding to your inquiries and requests
    • Managing and providing our services
    • Keeping your resume and professional information
    • Sending updates and news about our services
    • Understanding your preferences and improving our services
    • Tracking IP addresses and browsing habits for analytics
    • Using cookies to enhance your experience

    3. Commercial Communications

    By submitting your data, you consent to receive commercial communications from us. You can opt out at any time by sending an email to jon@jongodesign.com.

    4. How Long We Keep Your Data

    We retain your data:

    • As long as necessary to provide our services or fulfill our contract
    • For five years after the contract ends for legal and accounting purposes
    • Indefinitely for marketing purposes (with your consent and opt-out option)

    5. What Data You Need to Provide

    Required fields are mandatory to provide our services. Optional fields help us improve your experience.

    6. Will We Share Your Data?

    We do not share your data with third parties unless it's essential to provide the service you've requested.

    7. Your Rights

    You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

    • Access: Request information about what data we have about you
    • Correction/Update: Request correction of inaccurate data
    • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
    • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing
    • Limitation of Processing: Request restriction of data processing
    • Objection: Object to data processing
    • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

    To exercise these rights, contact us at jon@jongodesign.com or the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD).

    8. Cookies

    We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files that help us understand how you use our website. We use them in an anonymized form to improve our services.

    Most browsers accept cookies automatically, but you can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies if you prefer.
